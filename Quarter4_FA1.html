Why support this charity organization?

Polar bears are one of the many factors affected by climate change, their once cold and icy home, is now a warm environment where thungs are starting to melt. Another reason is because I feel remorse for the polar bear having to suffer due to the actions of humans, and if we dont save these poor animals, more will be affected just like them. So, I advocate for this chairty because I love polar bears, I want to help them with their struggles, and prevent more animals experiencing the same thing.
